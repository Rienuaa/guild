THE ADVENTURER'S GUILD

An incremental game, made by Ellen Cantor, based off of the book Mogworld by Yahtzee Crosshaw.

Gold:
Gold per second: 0









You have adventurers.
Adventurers cost 10 gold each. They earn 1 gold per second. You earn 0 per second.

You have parties.
Parties cost 100 gold and 10 adventurers each. They hire 2 adventurers per second. You earn 0 per second.

You have guild houses.
Guild Houses cost 1,000 gold and 100 parties each. They hire 3 parties per second. You earn 0 per second.

You have guild halls.
Guild Halls cost 10,000 gold and 1000 guild houses each. They build 4 guild houses per second. You earn 0 per second.

You have large halls.
Large Halls cost 100,000 gold and 10,000 guild halls each. They build 5 guild halls per second. You earn 0 per second.

You have territories.
Territories cost 1,000,000 gold and 100,000 large halls each. They hire 6 large halls per second. You earn 0 per second.

You have kingdoms.
Kingdoms cost 10,000,000 gold and 1,000,000 territories each. They hire 7 territories per second. You earn 0 per second.
